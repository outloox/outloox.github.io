Your complete guide to getting started with our site and services effectively and safely.

How It Works: 3 Simple Steps
  1. Browse the available accounts on the dashboard. Each card represents an account you can use.
  2. Click the 'Check Validity' button for your desired account. This sends a request to our intelligent bot.
  3. Our Telegram bot will instantly verify the account's status and send you the full details (including password and linked services) if it's active. This ensures you never get a bad account.
Safety & Best Practices

To ensure a smooth and fair experience for everyone, following these community guidelines is mandatory. These rules exist to protect the longevity of the accounts and the community.

  • Never change the password. This is the most important rule. Changing the password locks everyone else out and will result in an immediate and permanent ban from our service.
  • Do not store any personal or sensitive information on these accounts. They are shared, so assume anything you add can be seen by others. Use them for their premium features only.
  • All accounts come with full Outlook inbox access. Feel free to use this to sign up for other services or for email verifications, but do not use it for personal correspondence.
